Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 17, 2025

Professor ("App") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data.

1. Data We Collect

  • Account Information: Email address, full name
  • Usage Data: Number of solved questions, app usage statistics
  • Visual Content: Question photos submitted for solving
  • Payment Information: Purchase transactions (processed by Google Play/Apple)

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Account creation and management
  • Providing question solving service
  • Improving service quality
  • Providing technical support

3. Data Security

  • All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • We do not share your personal data with third parties
  • Your data is not disclosed except for legal requirements

4. Data Retention

  • Your data is retained while your account is active
  • Solution history is limited to the last 5 solutions
  • All your data is permanently deleted when your account is deleted

5. Your Rights

  • You can request access to your data
  • You can request correction of your data
  • You can request deletion of your account and data

6. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

7. Changes

This privacy policy may be updated. Significant changes will be notified through the app.
